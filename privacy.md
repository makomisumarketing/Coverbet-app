# Privacy Policy — CoverBet

**Last updated:** (add date at release)

---

## Overview

CoverBet is designed to keep your data on your device.  
No account is required to use the app.

---

## Information Stored

The app stores the following locally on your device:

- Parlay tickets and leg data you create  
- App settings and preferences (e.g., premium status, display options)

---

## Information We Do Not Collect

This version of the app does not intentionally collect or store:

- Name, email, or phone number  
- Precise location data  
- Contacts or personal identifiers  

> This may change if analytics, accounts, or additional third-party services are added in future versions.

---

## Third-Party Services

### Ads (Free Version)

CoverBet may display advertisements using third-party providers such as Google AdMob.

These providers may:

- Use device identifiers and cookies  
- Collect limited usage data  
- Use data for personalized or non-personalized advertising  

This data is handled directly by the ad provider and is subject to their privacy policies.

**Learn more:**
- https://policies.google.com/privacy
- https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

---

### Payments

If you purchase premium features, payment processing is handled by Apple (StoreKit).

- We do not receive or store your payment information  
- Apple may collect data in accordance with its own privacy policies

## Children’s Privacy

CoverBet is intended for adults and is not directed toward children.

## Your Control

You have full control over your data:

- Removing the app deletes all stored data
